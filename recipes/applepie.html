Steps

  1. Preheat oven: Prepare the oven to the temperature specified in your recipe.
  2. Grease and flour pan: This prevents the cake from sticking during baking.
  3. Combine dry ingredients: Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, and any other dry ingredients.
  4. Combine wet ingredients: Mix together eggs, milk, oil, and any other wet ingredients.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool: Let the cake cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
